Structuring vs. Validating

Yours to author. Yours to judge.

Two distinct steps — never blurred into one, and never done for you.

Structuring

You author the plan — Business Idea, Problem, Target Customer, Solution, Value Proposition — one guided question at a time.

Validating

You mark each assumption yourself — honestly, one at a time. Nothing is auto-confirmed.

○ "Customers will pay for this" — still assumption
✓ "The problem is real" — validated by you

"Validated" here always means you explicitly marked it — never an automated or third-party check.
